KiddoAI Tutor - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Overview

KiddoAI Tutor ("the App") is an educational tutoring application designed for children in grades K-6. We are committed to protecting children's privacy in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Information We Collect

Voice Input: When a child taps the microphone button, their speech is transcribed using on-device speech recognition (Android SpeechRecognizer). The transcribed text (not raw audio) is sent to Google's Gemini AI to generate tutoring responses.

Local Data: The App stores learning progress (grade level, exercise scores, streak counts, badges) in the device's local storage. This data never leaves the device.

No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, photos, or any other personally identifiable information.

How We Use Information

Transcribed text is sent to Google AI solely to generate age-appropriate educational responses. We do not store, log, or retain any conversation content on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Google AI (Gemini): Transcribed text is processed by Google's Generative AI API. Google's privacy policy applies to their processing of this data. No child names or identifiers are included in API requests.

We do not use any advertising services, analytics trackers, or social media integrations.

Data Storage & Security

All learning progress is stored locally on the device using browser localStorage. No child data is transmitted to or stored on external servers. The AI proxy server only forwards requests to Google AI and does not log or retain request content.

Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:

  • Review the data stored locally (accessible via the parent dashboard with PIN)
  • Delete all data by using the "Reset Session" button in the app or clearing the app's storage
  • Revoke consent at any time by clearing the app's data

Parental Consent

The App requires parental consent before first use. A parent or guardian must confirm their identity and agree to this privacy policy through the in-app consent screen.

Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of any material changes to this policy through the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or to exercise parental rights, contact us at: privacy@kiddoai.app
